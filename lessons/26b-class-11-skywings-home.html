💡 Design tip: The "View Packages" button repeats four times in the resorts grid — that's intentional. Repetition creates visual rhythm and tells the visitor "every resort works the same way; pick one." Don't be afraid of repeating a CTA.

Suggested Bootstrap Components

  • Hero — a large image with overlaid text and a button. Use a .container inside a hero div with a background image.
  • Resort cards — Bootstrap .card components in a .row with .col-md-6 or .col-lg-3.
  • Service blocks — three .col-md-4 columns with icons and short text.
  • Buttons — .btn .btn-primary or custom-styled buttons.
