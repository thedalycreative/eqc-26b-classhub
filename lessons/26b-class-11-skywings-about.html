Suggested Bootstrap Components

  • Intro — a .container with a centered <h1> and lead paragraph using .lead class.
  • Directors — three rows of .card with image-left, text-right layout. Use .row + .col-md-4 for image, .col-md-8 for bio.
  • Team grid — 6 .col-md-4 cards in two rows, each with a circular avatar image.
  • Services section — long-form copy split into 2-column layout for readability on wide screens.
